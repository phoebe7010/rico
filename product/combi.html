COMBI BLINDS

콤비 블라인드

Combi Blinds is a vertical style that use two different materials of fabric and mesh. It is capable of controlling the amount of light at a desired position. It is an innovative design product that can intercept the light and transmit the soft light while the mesh part and the fabric part are doubly intersected.
